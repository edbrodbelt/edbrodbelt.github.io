Embedded Systems Project GPS

This project was an individual Embedded Systems Project where we were given an Mbed Development Board and over the course of about 10 weeks, had to develop a programme to demonstrate our C++ skills. The brief stated that commonly people would make arcade games, data loggers etc.

Now I have never been much of a gamer, but have always enjoyed the outdoors, whether that be Mountain Biking, Skiing or Hiking, so I thought some project along those lines would ensure that I continued to develop it through out the full 10 weeks, because I would be interested in the topic.

In the end I settled on a GPS device that has a Clock, Compass, Map and Activity Tracker Functionality.
