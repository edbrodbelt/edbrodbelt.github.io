Power Electronics Command Line Based Calculator

As part of a group project I developed a Calculator that helps you answers questions related to the topic of Power Electronics.

The aim of the project was to come up with a technical engineering tool in groups of three and develop a command line based program.

My group settled on a Power Electroncis calculator and we each took a part of that topic and designed a tool to aid with that.
