Welcome to my 3D printing page!

As an avid user of the Prusa i3 MK3S+ with MMU2S printer, I've been able to create a wide range of useful items and prototypes. In fact, I'm proud to say that I currently own two of these printers, which allows me to produce high-quality prints in a timely manner.

In addition to my personal projects, I also sell 3D printed parts and designs on Etsy through my shop, 3D EB Designs. Some of the items I've printed and designed are included on this page, showcasing the variety of things that can be created with 3D printing technology.

Whether you're interested in custom designs, business inquiries, or simply want to discuss the possibilities of 3D printing, please see the "about" section for my contact details.
