Drink Plates

As someone who is passionate about strength training and has a love for rapid prototyping, I have combined my skills to design and create novelty gym gifts, mainly coasters inspired by weight plates. Through my Etsy shop, 3DEB Designs, I have developed my business and entrepreneurial skills by managing product testing, promotions, and dealing with shipping logistics. The process of designing and creating these items has also allowed me to refine my 3D CAD design skills, which is an area I am interested in pursuing further. Overall, it has been a fulfilling experience to turn my hobby into a business and see my designs being enjoyed by others who share a similar interest in fitness and weightlifting.
